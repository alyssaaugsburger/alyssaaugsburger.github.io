Work

Methane Anomaly Detector

Capstone project that identifies potential methane emission anomalies in California by applying deep learning techniques, specifically a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) Autoencoder, on data collected by Sentinel 5P, a satellite launched by the European Space Agency. This project included building a data pipeline to incorporate multiple data sources, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, and hyperparameter tuning. Our group built a web application that showcases near real-time data, anomaly detection, and data visualizations. Our team was award the Sarukkai Social Impact Award for this project, which recognizes the School of Information master’s capstone project with the greatest potential to solve important social problems and improve people’s lives in meaningful ways.

Question-Answering Task with Natural Language Processing Models

A natural language processing project that identifies the most relevant answer to Stack Overflow questions. Utilized different language embeddings, data ordering techniques and deep learning architectures to predict the most useful and appropriate answer. Embedded the data using the Universal Sentence Encoder (USE), Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT), Word2Vec and various combinations of these embeddings. Final model achieved a 26% increase in performance compared to the popular TFIDF BM-25 model.

Predicting Flight Departure Delays

Predicted flight departure delays using various machine learning algorithms: logistic regression, decision tree, random forest, and gradient boosted trees. Utilized the Databricks platform and Spark to process over 30 million rows (20+ GB) of data. One hot encoded categorical data, created new features, conducted Principal Component Analysis for dimensionality reduction, and hyperparameter tuned in order to create the best model.

Bike Share Data Analysis

Performed analysis on the Lyft Bay Wheels dataset to understand selected business questions. Utlized SQL and BigQuery within the Google Cloud Platform (GCP). Queries are made from a Jupyter Notebook through a virtual machine. Findings are presented using Pandas output tables and Matplotlib visualizations. The data analysis results are used to create ideas for promotions to increase ridership.

  • Report

Facebook Marketplace Experiment

Conducted a between-subjects randomized block design experiment to measure the potential treatment effect of image quality on bidding price for items sold on Facebook Markeplace. Utilized Qualtrics XM’s API for a pre-experimental survey and power analysis and then listed items for sale on Facebook Marketplace. Produced linear and log-linear multivariate regression models that showed statistical significance on the treatment variable, suggesting that a higher quality image leads to a higher bid for an item.

About

Hi, I'm Alyssa. I am a data scientist living in Arvada, Colorado. I have always enjoyed working with numbers and data. After earning my undergraduate degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Georgia Tech, I worked in various supply chain roles for 7 years. During this time I found that the projects I enjoyed working on the most were the ones that involved any sort of data analysis. I am passionate about analyzing data to gain insight and using this insight to turn data into actionable improvement projects. I wanted to be able to work with larger datasets, perform more in depth analyses, and be able to better utilize the data. This led me to pursuing a master’s degree in Data Science from UC Berkeley. I thoroughly enjoyed my experience in the Master of Information and Data Science program and had the opportunity to work with many great peers and advisors. This portfolio showcases many of the projects I completed while pursing my degree. I am now working at Oracle as a data scientist.

When I’m not working you can find me spending time with my husband and Golden Retriever, enjoying all that Colorado has to offer. I enjoy skiing, hiking, boating and traveling. I am also an avid college football fan and spend most Saturdays in the fall watching games.
